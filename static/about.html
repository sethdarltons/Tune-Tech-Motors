Our Journey

Tune-Tech motors began with a vision to redefine auto cars. In the year 2014, Fred Odhiambo turned a lifelong passion for cars into a mission to provide unparalleled automotive service. What started as a modest workshop in Lodwar has grown into a state-of-the-art service center, thanks to our dedication to excellence and the trust of our community.

What We Stand For

We believe in more than just fixing cars. We are committed to:

  • Integrity: Providing honest assessments and transparent pricing to our customers.
  • Quality: Using only the best parts and the latest technology to repair your car.
  • Customer Care: Ensuring every customer feels valued and heard.


Meet The Team

Our team is our pride. Each member brings a unique set of skills and a dedication to excellence. Together we are committed to delivering top-notch service and exceeding your expectations.

Commitment to Community

We are proud to be an integral part of the St.Terresa community. Our involvement includes:


  • Sponsorships: We support local teams and with financial aids.
  • Charity Drives: We partner with local organizations to give back to those in need.
  • Employment: Tune-Tech has provided employment oportunities for youths in the community.

Why Choose Us

Choosing Tune-Tech Motors means choosing:

  • Experience: Years of combined experience and continuous training make our technicians nothing but the best in repairing vehicles.
  • Trust: We have a reputation built on honesty and reliability.
  • Innovation: We are always staying ahead with the latest and the best in automotive technology.

Our Experties

From routine maintenance to intricate repairs, we offer a wide array of services to keep your vehicle at its best:

  • Preventative Maintenance: Oil changes, tire rotations, and fluid checks to keep your car running smoothly.
  • Diagnostics and Repairs: Utilizing cutting-edge diagnostic tools to accurately identify and fix issues.
  • Performance Tuning: Custom tuning to enhance your vehicle's performance.
